Dr. Andrews Releases 'Throw Like a Pro' app to reduce elbow injuries

Dr. James Andrews and Dr. Kevin Wilk have released the 'Throw Like A Pro' app, designed to help reduce the number of elbow injuries,specifically Tommy John surgery. They 'anticipate a 60% reduction in overuse related to baseball injuries in the coming years' if the app is used properly, according to the press release.
